How long does a project take?
Most projects land in the 6–10 week range from kickoff to launch. Larger platforms or those with complex AI integrations can take 12–16 weeks. We define the timeline clearly during scoping so there are no surprises.
Do you work with early-stage startups?
Yes — some of our best work has been with early-stage companies. The caveat: you need a clear problem statement and be ready to move. We're not a good fit for "we're still figuring out what we want to build."
What do you need from us to get started?
A brief on your business, your target customer, and what you're trying to achieve. Brand assets if you have them. And a decision-maker who can give feedback in a timely way. That's it — we handle the rest.
Can we do a retainer for ongoing work?
Absolutely. Many clients start with a project and move to a monthly retainer for ongoing development, optimization, and new features. Retainers start at $2,500/month.
Do you handle hosting and maintenance?
We deploy to your preferred infrastructure (Vercel, AWS, Cloudflare) and document everything. Post-launch maintenance is available as an add-on. We don't lock you into proprietary systems — you own your code.
What if we need changes after launch?
All projects include a 30–90 day support window (depending on tier). After that, we offer maintenance packages or can handle changes on an hourly basis. We're a long-term partner, not a one-and-done vendor.
